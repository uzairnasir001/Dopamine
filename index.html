No. The formula is designed to support restorative sleep — not sedation. Most customers report waking up feeling lighter, clearer, and more energized, not groggy.

Why 5mg of Melatonin?

Melatonin traditionally comes in pills, gummies, and tablets with 1mg-10mg dosages, taken orally and absorbed within an hour, but this can result in excessive absorption beyond safety limits.
